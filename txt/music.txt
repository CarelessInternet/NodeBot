Plays videos/music in user's voice channel

play (*required parameter*): Plays a video with the search query as parameter
volume (*required parameter*): Changes volume to the parameter, must be between 0-2, default is 0.5
queue: Shows the queue
skip: Skips the current video
remove: Removes a specified video from the queue
loop: Loops through current video
unloop: Removes the loop of the current video
pause: Pauses current video
resume/unpause: Resumes current video
leave: Leaves user's voice channel
